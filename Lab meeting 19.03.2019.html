Expression data for WormBase ParaSite

We will display quantification and alignment data from RNASeq-er (EBI project), curated and analysed on a per-study level.

Data offered

For all studies

These are all a simple aggregation of what RNASeq-er offers:

  • Characteristics and conditions per run
  • Raw data (counts of aligned reads) per run
  • Gene expression (TPM) per run
Where available
  • Gene expression (TPM) per condition as median across replicates
  • Differential expression analysis
