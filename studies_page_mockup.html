Intention

Add a scaled down and customised Expression Atlas, in its selected capabilities, to WormBase ParaSite.

  • process and curate studies
  • for each study, show “all results from this study”
  • for each gene, show “everything we have for the gene”
  • aggregate data for groups of genes
  • allow searching by biological condition
  • spark insight with charts and other visualisations

Data we can offer

We should pinch the data from RNASeq-er and then reshape it a bit. They offer: Kallisto, htseq2, TPM, FPKM per read.

Quantification

Proposed: TPMs from htseq2, median value per replicate group

Differential expression

DESeq2: annotate each sample such that they group into assays. DESeq2 will fit a distribution to the data, and then for each gene it will report how much of the variation can be attributed to differences in the groups. E.g. multiple strains, multiple genders - quantify the variation only due to strain, and only due to gender. Expression Atlas: just two groups, reference and test, in “differential experiments”

raw counts

Raw quantification data (counts) in a convenient file
